<p>Undoubtedly you know <span class='zawgyi'>ေရာ</span>– as a verb meaning ‘to mix’, but it is also used for identifying items in lists.</p>
<p><span class='zawgyi'>အမ်ားႀကီးပဲ။</span> There’s masses.</p>
<p class="hide-trigger"><a href='#'>+</a><span class='zawgyi'>စားစရာ အမ်ားႀကီးပဲ။</span></p>
<p class='hide-this'>There’s scads of food.</p>

<p class="hide-trigger"><a href='#'>+</a><span class='zawgyi'>ေရခဲမုန္႔ေရာ၊ စားစရာ အမ်ားႀကီးပဲ။</span></p>
<p class='hide-this'>There’s loads to eat: ice cream...</p>

<p class="hide-trigger"><a href='#'>+</a><span class='zawgyi'>သရက္သီးေရာ၊ ေရခဲမုန္႔ေရာ၊ စားစရာ အမ်ားႀကီးပဲ။</span></p>
<p class='hide-this'>There’s a ton of food: mangoes, ice cream....</p>

<p class="hide-trigger"><a href='#'>+</a><span class='zawgyi'>ေကာက္ၫွင္းေရာ၊ သရက္သီးေရာ၊ ေရခဲမုန္႔ေရာ၊ စားစရာ အမ်ားႀကီးပဲ။</span></p>
<p class='hide-this'>There’s heaps of to eat: sticky rice, mangoes, ice cream.</p>

<p class="hide-trigger"><a href='#'>+</a><span class='zawgyi'>မုန္႔ဟင္းခါးေရာ၊ ေကာက္ၫွင္းေရာ၊ သရက္သီးေရာ၊ ေရခဲမုန္႔ေရာ၊ စားစရာ အမ်ားႀကီးပဲ။</span></p>
<p class='hide-this'>There’s loads of food: mohinga, sticky rice, mangoes, ice cream.</p>

<p class="hide-trigger"><a href='#'>+</a><span class='zawgyi'>ငါးေျခာက္ေၾကာ္ေရာ၊ မုန္႔ဟင္းခါးေရာ၊ ေကာက္ၫွင္းေရာ၊ သရက္သီးေရာ၊ ေရခဲမုန္႔ေရာ၊ စားစရာ အမ်ားႀကီးပဲ။</span></p>
<p class='hide-this'>There’s masses of food: fried dried fish, mohinga, sticky rice, mangoes, ice cream.</p>
